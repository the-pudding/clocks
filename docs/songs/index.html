a clock where the time is in a song title

Created by Russell Samora.

Every minute, random songs are selected and played that contain the time in the title (e.g., 6:47 or 6:47 from Central Station).

All possible songs including times were queried using the Spotify API. Up to 10 were selected for each minute after sorting by a various factors (e.g., popularity, likelihood of time reference, US market, etc.).
